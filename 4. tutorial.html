@sapphire_wani's art tutorial, tips and tricks.

sketch
1. recognize the main shape of the objects. How many circles, squares, triangle are there.
2. sketch the main shape of the objects.
3. recognize small details of the object.
4. add the small details.

colour
1. recognize the base colour of the object. Usually the base colour is the colour of the object without any shadow or light effect.
2. colour the base colour of the object.
3. recognize the other colours existed on the object and its surrounding. the colours is not necessarily monotonous to the base colour.
4. colour the other colours.

tips
1. to achieve soft shading using pencils, rub the colour on paper using soft facial tissue.
2. take off your glasses to see which part of your drawing need to be fix.

tricks
-if manually drawing the image saved on phone, go to "crop" then tap it and quickly screenshot the image. the screenshot divided into 9 section, so divide the drawing paper into 9 section too to make it easier for you to draw the picture.
